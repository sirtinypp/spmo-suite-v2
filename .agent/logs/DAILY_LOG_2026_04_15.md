### 🔧 SSO Redirect Fix (14:30–16:00 PHT)
- **Issue**: Google SSO login resulted in `redirect_uri_mismatch` on staging/production.
- **Root Cause**: Cloudflare terminates SSL at the edge, and Nginx was passing `http` instead of `https` in the `X-Forwarded-Proto` header, causing Allauth to generate insecure callback URLs.
- **Fix Applied**: Hardcoded `X-Forwarded-Proto https` in `nginx/conf.d/default.conf` and ensured `USE_X_FORWARDED_HOST = True` in Django settings.
- **Result**: ✅ Verified. SSO now works perfectly via Cloudflare.
