Digital Procurement Process

Efficient, transparent, and paperless travel processing.

1. Submit Request

Fill out the GFA form online. Attach necessary documents like your Travel Order and signed RIS.

2. SSPMO Review

SSPMO admins review your request for compliance. You can track the status in real-time.

3. Receive Ticket

Once confirmed, the e-ticket is issued directly to your email and downloadable from the portal.
